Investigation Overview¶

The goal of this investigation is to understand the key features that drives the price of a car. You want to own a car company?, there sure are certain features you need to pay attention to. We will talk about some of these.

Dataset Overview¶

The Car Price Prediction DataSet was gotten from https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/deepcontractor/car-price-prediction-challenge/discussion/335871. The dataset presents the pricing of cars with some car features including 'Levy', 'Manufacturer', 'Model', 'Prod. year','Category', 'Leather interior', 'Fuel type', 'Engine volume', 'Mileage','Cylinders', 'Gear box type', 'Drive wheels', 'Doors', 'Wheel', 'Color','Airbags'.
